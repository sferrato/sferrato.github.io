About The Artist

Sierra Ferrato is a pictorial artist from San Jose, California who mainly works with acrylic paint on canvas. Her style varies from abstract line work to bright nature-inspired scenes. Through painting, Sierra has been inspired by nearby landscapes of the Bay Area and colorful, imaginary scenes. She started a small business, Art By Sierra, during the pandemic has been pursuing it ever since. ​

She is a full-time student at San Jose State University, pursuing a Studio Art degree, Preparation for Teaching. Her most recent works have been added into her portfolio that range from work within Photoshop and Illustrator to glitching images. ​
